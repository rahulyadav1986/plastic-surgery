Chin and Jaw Augmentation

Imagine looking in the mirror and feeling truly content with what you see, knowing that your outer appearance again reflects your inner self.

As we grow older, we lose the elasticity of our skin, and volume decreases from the underlying tissues of our face. This can be characterized by deep creases, displaced or excess fat deposits, loss of muscle tone and loose, sagging skin. This can make us appear older than we are, and most definitely older than we feel.

A facelift will help to correct these undesirable effects, and when performed by a skilled plastic surgeon, produces very natural and long-lasting results.

Why people have this procedure:

Tighten lax skin, eliminate jowls and improve facial tone.

Turn back the effects of time, and restore a more fresh, youthful appearance.

Increase confidence and self esteem, and feel more like themselves when they look in the mirror.

The Process

This surgical procedure addresses the underlying musculature and soft tissues of the face, neck and jaw, to "lift", restore and rejuvenate. Our surgeons have extensive and specialized experience, with a thorough knowledge of facial anatomy and underlying structures. This intensive training, expertise and skill ensures that you will look and feel your very best, as a more youthful version of yourself.

Vectra 3D technology is very useful during your pre-surgical consultation so that you can see the result that can potentially be achieved. After careful analysis, your surgeon will create a surgical plan based on your goals and their extensive experience, to achieve the natural and refreshed appearance that you desire.

During the procedure, an incision is made starting at the temples, traces downward to the ear and along the outer aspect of the tragus, wraps around the earlobe and ends at the lower hairli ne.These incisions are very well hidden in the hairline and natural facial structures.

  • Lax muscles are tightened to improve tone.
  • The skin is carefully re-draped, avoiding the dreaded "wind blown" a ppea rance.The emphasis is on enhancing, not "tightening."
  • The excess tissue is removed, and the incisions are closed with fine sutures.
For optimum results, a full facelift should also include a neck lift.

The Recovery

Optimal total recovery time is 2 months, but results will continue to improve over a year.

  • You will be able to return to your normal daily routine 10-14 days after your procedure.
  • The visible signs of surgery, such as bruising and swelling, usually resolve 3-4 weeks after surgery.
  • You can resume exercising in about 3 weeks.

Options/techniques we offer

Mini Facelift

Typically designed for younger patients, a mini facelift targets sagging skin around the cheek, "aw and jowl with less surgery than a full facelift for a subtle, lifted result. This procedure also requires a shorter recovery period.

Mid Facelift

This minimally invasive procedure lifts cheek tissue, resulting in a more youthful position while softening nasolabial folds. The midface lift is an excellent next step for patients who have previously relied on filler injections.

Nano Fat Transfer

As we age, we lose volume and the natural contours of our faces.This can be corrected by harvesting fat from another part of your body, purifying it, and then carefully positioning it into the target areas to re-contour and restore a more youthful look.
